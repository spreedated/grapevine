Greased Lightning

Grapevine is fast! A completely async request/response stack means I/O and network intensive processes execute outside of the primary application thread. This drastically improves application performance and enhances responsiveness.

100%

Your Monkeys, Your Circus

Grapevine is unopinionated. Hook in to the request/response pipeline where you want, when you want, and how you want. Serve files, expose an API, even call other services. You decide what makes sense for your solution.

100%

Lightweight and Low Impact

Grapevine is minimalist. It comes with only what you need, and without all the external dependencies that you don't need. Bulid in the features that you want, and don't worry about being limited by the features you don't.
